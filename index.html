Recipe #1

Cupcakes

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 10
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Eggs 2
Chocolate Chips 1 cup
Sugar 1 1/2 cups
Oil 1/2 cup
Cocoa powder 1 cup

Step 1: Pre-heat the oven to 180 degrees.

Step 2: Put all ingredients into a bowl and mix it up.

Step 3: Pour the mixture into individual cupcake moulds.

Step 4: Put the cupcake moulds into the oven.

Step 5: Keep it in the oven for 20-30 mins and Walla!

Recipe #2

Pasta and Cheese

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 1 1/2 bags
Cheese 1 bag
Salt 1/4 cup
Oil 1/4 cup

Step 1: Put pot on fire and heat water in it.

Step 2: When you notice the water is bubbling, put in the pasta.

Step 3: Let it boil in the pot for about 10 mins and add in salt and oil in the meantime.

Step 4: When you feel the Pasta is ready, drain the water from the pot and put the pasta into a tray.

Step 5: Add in the cheese and stir till it is mixed well, then taste it and Walla!

Recipe #3

Pizza on Pita

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Pita 1
Cheese 1/4 bag
Ketchup 1/19 bottle

Step 1: Take pita and spread Ketchup on it.

Step 2: Add some cheese onto your Ketchup.

Step 3: Put it in the oven for 5 mins.

Step 4: Take it out when it looks like the cheese has melted and enjoy!
